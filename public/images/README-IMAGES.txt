This folder needs 1 real photo to make the landing/login/register pages complete:

  warehouse.jpg  — a wide, well-lit photo of warehouse/inventory shelves
                   (used as the landing page hero image, and as the side
                   panel background on the login/register pages)

Where to get a free, real, no-license-needed photo:
  1. Go to https://unsplash.com
  2. Search: "warehouse shelves" or "warehouse inventory" or "logistics warehouse"
  3. Pick a photo that is landscape-oriented and reasonably bright
  4. Download it (free, no attribution required by Unsplash's license)
  5. Rename it to: warehouse.jpg
  6. Place it in this folder: public/images/warehouse.jpg

Once the file is in place, the landing page hero and the login/register
side panels will show it automatically — no code changes needed.
